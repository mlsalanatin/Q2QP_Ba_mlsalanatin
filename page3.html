Who We Are

At only, in my dreams, we are a passionate team of love advocates dedicated to fostering a world where love is not only celebrated but also actively shared as a transformative force. Guided by our deep appreciation for the beauty of love, we are committed to creating meaningful connections, offering insights through workshops, and engaging in diverse involvements that extend compassion to both individuals and communities. Our organization is founded on the belief that love has the power to inspire positive change, and we are devoted to nurturing a culture where acts of love, kindness, and understanding become integral to the human experience. Together, we strive to make the dream of a more loving world a reality for everyone we touch. At the core of only, in my dreams is a shared personal journey, as each team member has been profoundly touched by the transformative nature of love. Whether through the joy of falling in love for the first time or the resilience in sustaining lifelong partnerships, our collective experiences shape our advocacy. We recognize the complexities of love, embracing both its highs and lows, and stand united in our belief that fostering connections and promoting love as a universal language can bring about positive change. As a diverse team with a shared passion, we bring unique perspectives, skills, and a common mission to create a world where the warmth of love radiates across all boundaries, making a lasting impact on the lives we touch.

Vision

At only, in my dreams, our vision is to cultivate a world where love serves as a guiding force, transcending borders and fostering genuine connections among diverse individuals. Envisioning a society marked by empathy, understanding, and kindness, we strive to impart the transformative power of love to every corner of our communities. Our aspiration is to create an inclusive space where individuals embrace the beauty of both shared and individual love stories, and where the principles of compassion and respect form the foundation for thriving relationships. With an unwavering commitment to making love a universally cherished and celebrated experience, our vision propels us to inspire positive change, one heartfelt connection at a time.

Mission

At only, in my dreams, our mission is to spread the magic of love by creating a supportive platform that empowers individuals to build meaningful connections and nurture healthy relationships. We are dedicated to providing resources, insights, and interactive experiences through workshops, community engagement, and online initiatives. Our mission extends beyond individual connections to embrace a broader societal impact, promoting love as a catalyst for positive change. We aim to foster a culture where love is celebrated, relationships are strengthened, and empathy becomes a cornerstone of human interaction. By actively engaging in diverse involvements and collaborations, we seek to inspire a collective shift towards a more compassionate and interconnected world, where love becomes a guiding force for personal and societal well-being.
